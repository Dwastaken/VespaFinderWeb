Who are we?

From our passion to protect lives and nature in a unique way, we have specialized in innovative nature concepts over the past 10 years.

At Robor Electronics, we develop electronic applications and software to support people, animals, and nature. With every problem you encounter, we examine what we can do and what we can devise. Coming up with new things and innovative solutions is what we excel at. Innovation isn't just about new things, but also about smarter solutions. We aim to make workflows more efficient than yesterday.

Our specializations are drone technology and wireless communication systems. Within these specializations, we work with sensors, flying, detection, localization, and software development. For drones, we primarily work with the DJI brand, a large, reliable, and high-quality brand. We develop various applications and extensions for these drones ourselves, such as thermal cameras and software that makes the application more efficient.

In everything that exists, we see how it can be better, smarter, and more practical. So we get to work! How can it be more efficient and convenient, and how can we innovatively deploy your hardware that is currently being used?
